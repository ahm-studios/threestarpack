The Rise of Three Star Packaging Solutions

Three Star Packaging Solutions has emerged as a leader in Sri Lanka's flexible packaging industry, helping brands unlock the full potential of their packaging as a critical business asset. Our innovative approach combines advanced machinery and skilled craftsmanship to create packaging solutions that not only protect products but also enhance brand value and customer experience.

Advanced Manufacturing Capabilities

Through strategic partnerships and continuous innovation, we've developed packaging solutions that address the evolving needs of modern consumers while maintaining the highest standards of quality and sustainability. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted partner for leading brands across multiple industries including tea exports, apparel, spices, and poultry.

Comprehensive Product Range

From HDPE and LDPE extrusions to laminated pouches (PET, METPET, BOPP, CPP), our solutions are designed to meet the most demanding requirements while providing cost-effective and environmentally responsible options. This comprehensive approach has positioned Three Star Packaging as a leader in helping brands harness the full potential of their packaging investments.

Quality Assurance and Innovation

Our state-of-the-art facility in Colombo houses over 40 skilled professionals dedicated to maintaining the highest quality standards. We invest heavily in research and development to stay ahead of industry trends and deliver innovative solutions that meet global standards.

Future Vision

As we continue to grow, Three Star Packaging Solutions remains committed to expanding our capabilities, embracing new technologies, and strengthening our position as Sri Lanka's premier flexible packaging manufacturer. Our vision is to become the go-to partner for businesses seeking reliable, innovative, and sustainable packaging solutions.

An Overview of Three Star Packaging Solutions

Get a comprehensive understanding of Three Star Packaging Solutions' mission, capabilities, and commitment to excellence in the packaging industry.

Our Company Story

Three Star Packaging Solutions stands as a testament to innovation, quality, and customer-centric service in Sri Lanka's flexible packaging industry. Established in 2008, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional packaging solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients across multiple industries.

Comprehensive Service Range

Our comprehensive range of services encompasses everything from initial concept development to final product delivery, ensuring that every aspect of the packaging process is handled with precision and care. We work closely with our clients to understand their unique requirements and develop customized solutions that exceed expectations.

Quality Assurance and Standards

Quality assurance is at the heart of everything we do, with rigorous testing protocols and continuous improvement processes ensuring that our products meet the highest industry standards. Our commitment to innovation drives us to constantly explore new technologies and materials that can enhance our offerings and provide greater value to our clients.

Industry Expertise and Specialization

With over 15 years of experience, we've developed deep expertise in serving Sri Lanka's leading tea exporters, as well as clients in apparel, spices, poultry, and other sectors. Our specialized knowledge of these industries allows us to provide tailored solutions that address specific challenges and requirements.

Production Capabilities and Technology

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced machinery for HDPE, LDPE, and PP extrusions, as well as sophisticated lamination processes for PET, METPET, BOPP, and CPP materials. This technological advantage enables us to produce high-quality packaging solutions that meet international standards.

Team and Future Vision

Our dedicated team of over 40 professionals brings together expertise in engineering, design, quality control, and customer service. As we look to the future, Three Star Packaging Solutions remains committed to expanding our capabilities, embracing new technologies, and strengthening our position as Sri Lanka's premier flexible packaging manufacturer.
