OUR HISTORY

Established in 2008, Three Star Packaging Solutions is a trusted manufacturer of flexible packaging materials in Sri Lanka. With advanced machinery and a skilled team, we deliver customized packaging solutions across multiple industries.

We are proud to serve Sri Lanka's leading tea exporters, along with clients in apparel, spices, poultry, and other sectors. Our production capabilities include HDPE, LDPE, PP extrusions, and laminated pouches (PET, METPET, BOPP, CPP), enabling us to offer innovative, high-quality packaging.

With a dedicated team of over 40 professionals, we support both export and local markets, delivering reliable, value-added packaging tailored for food and fashion industries.
